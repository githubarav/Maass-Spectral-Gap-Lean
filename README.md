Maass Form Spectral Gap Formalization in Lean 41. OverviewThis repository provides a top-down formal architecture for the 63/256 spectral gap bound for Maass forms on congruence subgroups. This work explores the logical bridge between the Kim-Shahidi Symmetric 9th Power Lift results and the spectral parameter $\theta \le 7/64$.2. Mathematical MethodologyThe project formalizes the deduction that the non-vanishing of the Symmetric 6th power L-function at the central point $s=1/2$ (or its behavior in the vanishing case) forces a specific lower bound on the first non-zero eigenvalue $\lambda_1$.The Target: $\lambda_1 \ge \frac{63}{256} \approx 0.246$.The Tools: Langlands Functoriality, Hecke Algebra, and the Selberg Trace Formula logic.3.
